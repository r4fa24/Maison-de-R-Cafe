Maison de R



About Us

Maison de R is where timeless elegance meets the art of coffee — in the heart of Downtown Dubai. Inspired by the charm of Parisian cafés, we’ve created a space where calm and comfort blend beautifully with modern city life. From our handcrafted signature blends to our warm, thoughtful service, every detail invites you to slow down and savour the moment. Step inside, breathe in the aroma, and make yourself at home.
